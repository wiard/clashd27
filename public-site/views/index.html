How It Works
🔍
Sample
Papers from arXiv, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and PubMed are ingested daily across domains.
🎲
Collide
A 3×3×3 cube classifies papers by method, surprise, and domain — then crashes distant cells together.
🔬
Verify
Multi-layer pipeline scores, screens, deep-dives, and validates each collision before publishing.
📣
Publish
Surviving gaps are published with evidence, proposed experiments, and scored for reproducibility.
Sources: arXiv, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, PubMed/EuropePMC
